HTN Project 2019

Curate your spending decisions based on similar TD customers

How to use the app

  1. Enter your TD credentials
  2. Drop a pin to an area where you want to purchase
  3. Select what type of products/services you are looking for (food, leisure, etc.)
  4. Enter your spending preferences and click submit
  5. Watch as our algorithm finds places near to you where users with similar spending preferences have used
